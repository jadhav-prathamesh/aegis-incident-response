# Slide 6: Live Demo

## Content

**End-to-End Incident Automation**

1. **Trigger Incident** → POST /incidents (critical severity)
2. **Auto-Detection** → Observer checks services, metrics, alerts
3. **AI Reasoning** → Orchestrator analyses, decides next step
4. **Plan Generation** → Planner searches KB, creates remediation steps
5. **Approval Workflow** → Human-in-the-loop for critical actions
6. **Automated Execution** → Executor dispatches actions with rollback
7. **Validation** → Validator runs 6 checks to confirm fix

**Demo Time:** ~12 minutes
