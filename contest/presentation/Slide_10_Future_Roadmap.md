# Slide 10: Future Roadmap

## Content

**Priority Roadmap**

**Immediate (Next Sprint)**
- Authentication middleware (JWT)
- Persistent incident store (PostgreSQL)
- CI/CD pipeline (GitHub Actions)

**Short-Term (2 Sprints)**
- Cloud SDK integration (AWS, GCP, Azure)
- Kubernetes operator
- ServiceNow integration

**Medium-Term (Quarter)**
- Learning & feedback loop
- Predictive failure detection
- Performance benchmarks

**Long-Term**
- ML pipeline (model fine-tuning)
- Chaos engineering
- Multi-tenancy
- Compliance certifications

## Design Notes

- 2×2 priority matrix:
  - High Impact / Low Effort → Do First
  - High Impact / High Effort → Invest
  - Low Impact / Low Effort → Quick Wins
  - Low Impact / High Effort → Avoid
- Each item in its quadrant with timeline label

## Speaker Notes

"We've prioritized the roadmap carefully. Authentication and persistent storage are the most critical items — they're needed for any production deployment. The learning loop will complete the agentic cycle, enabling the system to improve from every incident it handles."
